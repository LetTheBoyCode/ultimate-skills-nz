Support & FAQs

We have programs tailored for children aged between 9 - 13 years old. We also provide custom programs get in touch to find out more.
Your child's safety is our priority. Please inform us of any medical conditions, allergies, or physical limitations via email (All medical information is handled discreetly and shared only with relevant staff). It is vital that our instructors are aware of any health needs before the first session begins.
Your child should come prepared with athletic clothes, sun protection, closed-toe sneakers/boots, and a labeled water bottle. If the day requires specific gear (like shin pads or month guards), we will notify you in advance. Otherwise, we provide all necessary equipment!
We understand that plans change! If you need to cancel your registration, please notify us at least 48 hours before the program begins to receive a full credit toward a future session. Please note that we are unable to offer refunds or credits for individual missed days due to illness or personal scheduling conflicts.
No, food is not provided. Please pack a suitable lunch, snacks, and a refillable water bottle for your child to enjoy.
