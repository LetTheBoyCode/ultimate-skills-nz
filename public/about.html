Our Mission

Our mission is to unlock every athlete's potential through personised, evidence based coaching that emphasizes technical proficiency, physical conditioning and mental toughness across multiple sports. We envision a network of empowered young Kiwi's contributing to New Zealand's vibrant sports culture, with clear progression pathways from community participation to regional and national levels. By integrating innovative training methodologies with values like team work and perseverance, Ultimate Skills NZ aims to be the leading multi-sport academy for aspiring athletes in a nation passionate about diverse sports

Meet Head Coach Bmac

Director of Coaching Brendon McKeown is a distinguished sports professional with a Bachelor's degree in Sport and Recreation (Exercise Science) and a Postgraduate Diploma in Secondary Teaching. With extensive experience as a coach, educator, and sports leader, Brendon combines academic knowledge with hands-on expertise to develop athletes and teams to their fullest potential.

  • World Rugby Level 3 Coach, World Rugby Coach Educator, World Rugby Trainer.
  • Former Secondary Schools Director of Sport and Head of Physical Education and Health.

The "Ultimate Skills" Method

We believe that the best way to learn is through doing. Our sessions are designed to keep kids moving, laughing, and solving problems together.

  • Focus on Effort, not just Results
  • Inclusive Environment
  • Safe to Fail, Safe to Learn
