Our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions

Privacy Policy

At Ultimate Skills NZ, we take the privacy of your family seriously. This policy explains how we handle personal information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

Information We Collect

  • Parent/Guardian Details: Name, email, phone number, and address.
  • Child Details: Name, age, and any relevant medical information or allergies.
  • Payment Info: We use secure third-party processors and do not store credit card details on our servers.

Why We Collect It

We collect this data to safely run our programs, contact you in an emergency, and send you occasional updates about upcoming holidays. We never sell or rent your data to third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of the information we hold about you or ask us to delete it at any time. Just drop us an email at our contact page.

Terms & Conditions

By enrolling your child in an Ultimate Skills NZ program, you agree to the following terms:

1. Cancellations & Refunds

We understand plans change. Notify us at least 48 hours before a program begins to receive a full credit for a future session. We do not offer refunds or credits for individual missed days due to illness or personal scheduling conflicts.

2. Health & Safety

While we take every precaution to ensure a safe environment, sports involve physical movement and inherent risks. By enrolling, you acknowledge these risks. In an emergency, you authorize our staff to seek medical attention for your child if you cannot be reached.

3. Media Consent

We occasionally take photos for our social media. If you do not want your child photographed, please let us know in writing via email before the program starts.

4. OSCAR Subsidies

If you are applying for OSCAR subsidies, it is the parent's responsibility to ensure all Work and Income (WINZ) paperwork is submitted on time.
